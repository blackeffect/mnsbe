About Our Association

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) at Minnesota State University, Mankato is a vibrant and dynamic organization known for its commitment to academic excellence, cultural empowerment, and community engagement. As one of the most active and impactful student-led organizations on campus, NSBE creates meaningful opportunities for students to grow as engineers, scientists, technologists, and problem-solvers. Our chapter was honored as the Outstanding RSO of the Year at the 2025 Student Leadership Awards, a testament to our dedication and influence both on campus and in the greater Mankato community.

The NSBE Mankato chapter is dedicated to upholding the mission at Minnesota State University, Mankato and within the local community. We strive to create a supportive environment for our members, fostering academic excellence and providing opportunities for networking, skill-building, and community engagement. Our goal is to empower the next generation of Black leaders in STEM fields.

Why Join NSBE Mankato?

Becoming a member of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) Mankato Chapter offers unparalleled opportunities for growth, support, and success. Here are just a few reasons why you should join our thriving community:

Professional & Career Growth

Access exclusive career fairs, internship opportunities, resume workshops, and mock interviews. Connect with industry leaders and secure your path to a successful engineering career.

Academic Support & Excellence

Benefit from tutoring sessions, study groups, and scholarship opportunities designed to help you excel in your coursework and achieve academic success.

Strong Community & Networking

Join a supportive family of like-minded students and professionals. Build lasting friendships, find mentors, and expand your professional network within the engineering field.

Leadership & Skill Development

Take on leadership roles within the chapter, develop project management skills, and enhance your communication abilities through various initiatives and events.

Community Outreach & Impact

Participate in impactful STEM initiatives, volunteer events, and pre-college programs that inspire the next generation of engineers and give back to the community.

National Convention & Events

Attend regional and national conventions, conferences, and competitions, offering unparalleled exposure, learning, and networking opportunities on a larger scale.
