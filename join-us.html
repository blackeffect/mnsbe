Join NSBE Mankato Chapter

Take these steps to become an official member of the NSBE Mankato community:

  1. Visit Mav Central.
  2. Click "Student/Faculty/Staff Sign In" or sign in as a Guest or Community Partner.
  3. Navigate to Groups in the top menu.
  4. Search for “National Society of Black Engineers”.
  5. Click Join Group, then confirm by clicking Join.
