Our Story

Welcome to the Mankato NSBE Chapter! We are a student-run chapter of the National Society of Black Engineers, committed to empowering aspiring Black engineers, scientists, and technologists on our campus and in our community.

Our chapter provides academic support, leadership opportunities, career development, and a strong community for Black students in STEM. We host workshops, networking events, mentorship programs, and more to help members thrive inside and outside the classroom.

Our Mission

To increase the number of culturally responsible black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community.

Our Vision

To see a world where Black students are empowered to pursue engineering, thrive in academic and professional spaces, and bring engineering into the hearts of their families and communities.

Meet Our Leadership

At NSBE-Mankato chapter, The knowlegde of self is the heart of our success. We are go-givers—driven by purpose, service, and unity. Our leaders sets the tone, and together we work to empower each other and create lasting impact in our communities.

We are proud of our collaborative culture, which encourages continuous development and mutual respect. Each member contributes uniquely to our collective achievements, driving us forward to meet new challenges and exceed expectations. Our leadership team is committed to guiding our efforts with strategic vision and a deep understanding of our mission. They champion our values and ensure that every project we undertake aligns with our overarching goals of excellence and impact.
